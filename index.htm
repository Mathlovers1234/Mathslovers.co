Q1)Two tankers contain 850L and 680L of petrol respectively.
Find the maximum capcity of a container which can measure the petrol of either tanker, in exact number of times?


Answer)Given capacity of two tankers are 680L and 850L.
Here, 850>680
850=(680X1)+170
Here, remainder is not equal to 0, hence new divisor is 170.
Now, 680=(170X4)+0
so HCF of 850 and 680 is 170
Hence, the maximum capacity of required container is 170L.

Q4) The length, Breadth and height of a room are 8m 25cm,6m 75cm and 4m 50cm respectively
Find the length of the longest Rod that can measure the three dimensions of the room exactly.


Answer)Given,length of room is 8m 25cm=825 cm (1m=100cm)
Given,Breadth of room is 6m 75cm=675cm
Given,Height of room=4m 50cm=450cm
The length of the longest rod would be HCF of 825cm, 675cm, 450cm
Using Prime Factorization, We get;
825=3×52×11
675=33×52
450=2×32×52
Now HCF(825,675,450)=3×52=75
Hence, The length of the rod would be 75cm

Q1)A forester wants to plant 66 mango trees, 88 mango trees, 110 apple trees
in equal rows (in terms of number of trees).Also, he wants to make distinct rows of trees (i.e only one type of trees in each row. Find the number of minimum rows.

Answer)
Prime factors of 66=2×3×11

Prime factors of 88=23×11

Prime factors of 110=2×5×11

Now, HCF(66,88,110)=22

Now, Required no. of rows=66/22+88/22+110/22
=12

Q3)The sum of two numbers is 528 and their HCF is 33.Find the number of pairs satisfying this condition


Answer)Let the required number be 33x and 33y
Then,33x+33y=528-----> 33(x+y)=528
x+y=16
Now coprimes with sum 16 are (1,15)(3,13)(5,11)(7,9)
Required numbers are (33×1+33×15)(33×3+33×13)(33×5+33×11)(33×7+33×9)
The number of pairs are 4.

Q4)if √ab is an irrational number, prove that (√a+√b) is an irrational number

√a+√b=r; where a and b are rational numbers
Squaring Both Sides,
(√a+√b)2=r2
a+b+2√ab=r2      [(a+b)2=a2+b2+2ab]
2√ab=r2-a-b ------>√ab=[r2-a-b]/2
Since, r,a and b are Rational numbers
[r2-(a+b)]/2 is rational number
√ab is rational number
But,√ab is an irrational number
So,(√a+√b) is an irrational number
